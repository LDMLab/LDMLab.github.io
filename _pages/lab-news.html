Upcoming Workshops

Multilevel Modeling Workshop hosted by Sean

Multilevel models are statistical models of parameters that vary at more than one level. Sean is hosting a free workshop about conducting them in R!

June 29th, 2021 from 9:00 – 11:00am: Introduction to MLM

July 6th, 2021 from 9:00 – 11:00am: Advanced MLM

Interested in participating? Send the organizers a message

JavaScript for psychology hosted by Nathalie

A tutorial in collaboration with CUPA designed to show undergraduates and graduate students in psychology how to conduct simple behavioral and cognitive experiments using the jsPsych library.

July 9th, 2021: Introduction to jsPsych and Cognition.run

Check the tutorial repository for more information.

2018

Science of Learning Symposium in Berlin, Germany

Organised by Ben Eppinger, Julia Rodriguez Buritica, Dietsje Jolles, & Wouter van den Bos, the aim of this symposium was to lay out the different perspectives on science of learning research in education and developmental cognitive neuroscience and to crystalize challenges and gaps in both fields where an interdisciplinary exchange would be helpful.
